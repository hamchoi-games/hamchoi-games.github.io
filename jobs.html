Join Our Team at HamChơi Games

HamChơi Games is a growing Roblox studio focused on building popular games for a global audience. We're preparing to launch a major new title and are looking for talented full-time team members to help shape the future of our studio. We operate remotely but plan to open a physical office in Vietnam soon. While we welcome global applicants, we’re especially excited to connect with Vietnamese developers, artists, and creators who want to build the next big Roblox hit.

A1. Roblox Engineer

We’re hiring a full-time Roblox developer to lead game development for our upcoming title. You’ll be building gameplay systems, collaborating on design, and owning features from prototype to live deployment. You’ll also help define the long-term technical direction of our studio.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain core gameplay features using Lua and Roblox Studio
  • Build UI systems, player progression, and scalable multiplayer mechanics
  • Collaborate with artists and designers to iterate on game features
  • Debug and optimize for mobile and low-end devices
  • Contribute to architecture, coding standards, and best practices

Qualifications

  • 1+ years experience developing Roblox games
  • Ability to work full-time (40 hours per week)
  • Strong knowledge of Lua, OOP, and modular system design
  • Experience with Git or other version control systems
  • Strong communication and self-management skills
  • Bonus: Experience launching or maintaining a live Roblox game

Type: Full-Time

Compensation: $1200–$1600 USD/month

A2. 3D Artist (Roblox / Blender)

We’re looking for a full-time 3D artist to create environments, buildings, and props for a large-scale Roblox game. You’ll help define the visual identity of our game world

Responsibilities

  • Match our target visual style across all models
  • Create optimized, low-poly assets for houses, interiors, props, and environment pieces
  • Collaborate with programmers and designers to test and place models in-game
  • Ensure assets are Roblox-ready with proper scale, orientation, and mobile optimization

Qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience developing Roblox games
  • Ability to work full-time (40 hours per week)
  • Strong proficiency in Roblox Studio and Blender (AutoCAD is a plus)
  • Attention to scale, visual consistency, and player readability
  • Familiarity with the aesthetic and building style of games like Brookhaven, Berry Avenue, or Redcliff City RP
  • Ability to recreate that style through low-poly modeling, UV mapping, and asset optimization workflows

Type: Full-Time

Compensation: Above-market salary
